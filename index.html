About

I am a data scientist specialized in geospatial applications. I graduated from the Master in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from Maastricht University in 2024, with a thesis that looked at the optimization of hybrid Quantum-Classical GANs for image generation, under the supervision of Menica Dibenedetto. Before my Data Science specialization, I pursued a Bachelor in Human Ecology from College of the Atlantic, USA, providing me with a solid background in the environmental and climate sciences domain.

I am proficient in Python, SQL, R, and ML (scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow) and Geospatial packages (GeoPandas, Shapely, GDAL, QGIS).
